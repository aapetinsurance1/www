Pet insurance needs are personal. If you treat your beloved dog like a member of the family and you want to make sure that they get the best medical treatment, you would choose more comprehensive and more expensive insurance. On the other hand, plenty of people love their pets but truly can't pay thousands of dollars that will accumulate over the years in pet insurance premiums. In this situation you might want to opt out from pet insurance or settle for a cheaper plan.

Below summary of best pet insurance choices* for your dog should help your make the decision.
